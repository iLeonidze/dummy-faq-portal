Private runbook

Verification checklist for the demo service

  1. Open / and verify that the route map renders.
  2. Open /faq.html, /getting-started.html, and /operations.html directly and confirm they do not 404.
  3. Hit /healthz and verify that the response is 200 OK.
  4. Open /private/ and verify that nginx requests credentials.
  5. Authenticate with test/test and confirm that /private/status.html and /private/runbook.html render normally.
